RIDDLER

Rules & Instructions

  • The aim of Riddler is to go to the next level. By hook or by crook.
  • Decipher the answer from the clues given in each level.
  • Enter your answer into the textbox at the bottom of the page.
  • You are free to use any resource from the internet, that might help you solve a question.
  • You can check your personal progress and current rank by clicking on the Leaderboard button at the top.
  • Everything shown in the questions/pictures might not be a clue. We promise to make conscious efforts to mislead you.
  • There is only one winner. Rankings are assigned based on the chronological order in which players arrive at a level.
  • In case of any dispute or discrepancy, the decision taken by CSI will be final.
